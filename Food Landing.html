Our Special Food

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful dish featuring tender chicken pieces cooked with aromatic basmati rice, a blend of spices, and herbs. This layered and richly seasoned delicacy is garnished with fried onions and fresh coriander, offering a delicious and satisfying meal.

Veg Thali is a delightful platter featuring an assortment of flavorful vegetarian dishes. It typically includes fragrant rice, fresh roti or naan, a variety of curries, savory dal, tangy pickles, refreshing raita, and a sweet dessert. Perfect for experiencing a balanced taste of Indian cuisine.

Chilli Chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese dish featuring tender, boneless chicken pieces stir-fried with vibrant bell peppers, onions, and green chilies in a spicy, tangy sauce. Perfectly balanced with bold flavors, it offers a delightful kick in every bite.
